Benefits of Farm Chicken


1. Higher Nutritional Value

Lean Protein: Fresh farm chicken is a rich source of high-quality, lean protein, essential for muscle growth, repair, and overall health.

Better Fat Profile: It often contains a better balance of fats, including higher levels of beneficial omega-3 fatty acids, compared to conventionally raised chicken.

Vitamins and Minerals: Fresh farm chicken can have higher levels of vitamins and minerals, such as vitamin B6, vitamin B12, niacin, and selenium.
